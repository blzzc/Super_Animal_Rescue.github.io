About Our Team

What we do

As a member of the Super Animal Rescue Program, each of us has dedicated to raising awareness about stray animal rescue and promoting responsible adoption in different way.

We collaborate with Ripajiwan Animal Hospital and other community organizations to spread knowledge through social media campaigns and offline workshops.

By documenting real-life rescue experiences and engaging with the public, we hope to make a meaningful impact and help more stray animals find loving homes.
