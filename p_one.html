Interview

We conducted community interviews to gather perspectives and stories from ordinary citizens about stray animals, ensuring a diverse range of content. Additionally, we interviewed animal rescue experts to gain professional insights. Alongside expert views, we collected personal rescue stories from our team members, transforming these real-life experiences into compelling content to help the public emotionally connect with stray animals. All interview content was edited and published in video or article format, sharing it with a wider audience.
