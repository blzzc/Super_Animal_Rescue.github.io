Social Media and Awareness Campaign

We have developed a social media strategy to promote our website and its content, encouraging community members to participate and share information. We will also leverage local media channels to increase the visibility of the project and attract more attention to stray animal rescue. Social media platforms will serve as a bridge for interacting with the community, enhancing their sense of involvement and inspiring them to contribute to the cause of stray animal rescue.
